Quantum Physics Basics
=========================



Two Level Systems
------------------


In general a quantum two level system can be described using Schrodinger equation

.. math::
   i\partial_t \begin{pmatrix}
   \psi_1\\
   \psi_2
   \end{pmatrix}= \frac{1}{2}
   \begin{pmatrix}
   -\omega_0 & 0 \\
   0 & \omega_0
   \end{pmatrix}
   \begin{pmatrix}
   \psi_1\\
   \psi_2
   \end{pmatrix},

where :math:`\omega_0` is real.
