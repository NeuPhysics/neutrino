Preliminary
======================


This chapter is about the preliminary knowledge required by this topic.

Here are some facts about standard model neutrinos.

* They only participate in weak interactions.
* There are three flavors.
* They are light, but at least two of them have masses.
* Neutrinos are left-handed.
