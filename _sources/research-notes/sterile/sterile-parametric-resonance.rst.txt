Sterile-active Parametric Resonance
=====================================


Region of Interest
----------------------------

We need to know the matter density profile of a supernova at the time of interest.

We can do this for different time of explosion.



Sterile-active Parametric Resonance
--------------------------------------

**Parameters**

1. Mass of sterile neutrinos: :math:`m_s \sim 1\mathrm{eV}`
2. Mass squared difference: :math:`\Delta m \sim 1\mathrm{eV}`


Single Frequency Matter Potential
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

For matter profile

.. math::
   \lambda(r) = \lambda_0 + A\sin(k r).

Resonance condition is

.. math::
   nk\sim \omega_m.


To be specific,

.. math::
   n k \sim \frac{ \Delta m^2 }{2E} \sqrt{ ( \lambda_0/\omega_v - \cos(2\theta_v) )^2 + \sin^2(2\theta_v) }.

What would be the relavent region of matter density? We need to find out :math:`\lambda_0` as function of energy :math:`E`.
